Extreme Flavor. Zero Apologies.

Made for True Heat Lovers

Born straight out of the bustling street-food culture of , Tamil Nadu, Aakooo Eatzz was created to satisfy the ultimate spice craving. We take the iconic, crunchy Thattu Vadai Set (crispy flat puris loaded with grated carrots, beetroots, and hot chutneys) and give it an explosive modern twist.

Whether you want the crisp crunch of raw mangoes, the rich luxury of scrambled eggs (Mutta Norukkal), or the bold combination of spicy chicken and crushed puris, we serve it hot, fresh, and unapologetically spicy. We don't tone down the heat — we dial it up!
